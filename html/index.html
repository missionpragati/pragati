Empowering Dreams Mission Pragati

"Mission Pragati, a dedicated NGO for education, extends its unwavering support to individuals from economically disadvantaged backgrounds, state board education, and Hindi medium, with a special focus on nurturing aspirations for IITs, NITs, and NEET exams, ensuring that every deserving student has the opportunity to achieve their academic dreams."
